
Project Team
============

The team is jointly formed by members of Bosch BrgP and the University of Minho,
within a partnership model that is fully aligned with one of the key objectives
of the programme:

    Promote and strengthen the relationship between Bosch BrgP and the
    University of Minho, within their strategic and complementary R&D&I
    partnership, aimed at the development of advanced solutions for the
    automobile of the future.
